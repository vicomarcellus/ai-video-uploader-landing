Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

AI Art Uploader is a personal content management tool for automated artwork publishing. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

When you use our app, we may process:

  • Social media account tokens (for posting on your behalf)
  • Images you upload for publishing
  • AI-generated metadata for your content
  • Publishing history and basic analytics

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Generate AI-powered metadata for your images
  • Display analytics and publishing history within the app

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers except when communicating with social media APIs (Pinterest, TikTok) and OpenAI for metadata generation.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with:

  • Pinterest API — for content publishing
  • TikTok API — for content publishing
  • OpenAI API — for metadata generation
  • Google Drive API — for image storage

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their use of your data.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing the app's local database files or revoking access tokens from your social media accounts.

Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
